Vrecoil Project

What is the Vrecoil Project?

       The VRecoil project is a military simulation project that allows soldiers or civilians to enhance their close-quarters combat aiming, reflexes, and muscle memory skills. Its focus on realism sets it apart from similar initiatives in the same field.

       In my opinion, products aimed at this market underemphasize the value of realism. In order to close this gap, I place a high priority on the principle of realism and am developing our system with it in mind. Additionally, I am well aware of the importance of data and statistics because my project aims to benefit this industry. For a military employing this technology, having access to statistics both during and after the training process will be of great value.
